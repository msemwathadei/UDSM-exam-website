Examination Rules & Regulations

Please read carefully before attempting any examination

Please review the following rules carefully. Failure to comply with these regulations may lead to disciplinary action by the University.

    • Eligibility: Only students duly registered for a course are permitted to sit for its examination.
    • Identification: Students must present valid university identification or use official credentials.
    • Attendance: Students must be present or logged in before the examination begins.
    • Time: All examinations must be completed within the allocated time.
    • Integrity: Cheating, impersonation, collusion, or plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
    • Electronic Devices: Use or possession of unauthorized devices, including mobile phones, is not allowed.
    • Communication: Communication with other candidates during the examination is prohibited.
    • Online Exams: Students are responsible for reliable internet access and a suitable environment.
    • System Abuse: Any attempt to disrupt or gain unauthorized access to the system is prohibited.
    • Discipline: Any misconduct may result in disciplinary action in accordance with UDSM regulations.

Note: These are general examination rules. For detailed and official student rules and regulations, please visit the University of Dar es Salaam website .
